BASIC INSTRUCTIONS


Movement:
	left arrow => move left
	right arrow => move right
	z => sprint
	spacebar => jump

Objectives:
	Kill Enemies by jumping on their head
	Avoid gettings hit by enemies and traps, one hit will kill you
	Kill enemies and collect coins to gain points
	Gain a life every 1000 points
	Try to get the high score!

	
Complete level by jumping through portal
